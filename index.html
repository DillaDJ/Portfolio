About me!

Thanks for taking a moment to visit my website, hopefully you like what you see! I was born in South Africa and I lived there until 2011, which is when my family and I moved to New Zealand. I've been programming since I was 10 years old and I have a keen interest in graphics and software in general, I think a long term goal of mine would be to work my way into becoming a technical artist but the learning curve is very steep. I've been keeping my learning in the technical art and gamedev field as a hobby and maybe someday it'll be a carreer option, but for now I'm going to keep learning.

I studied Computer Science and Linguistics at the University of Auckland (2017 - 2023), I know it's quite the mix but I found Linguistics interesting so I did it on the side, it's great fun. I don't mind getting my hands dirty, that is to say, learning new things and adding them to my toolkit as a developer. Which is why I'll jump around and make projects in all sorts of engines, frameworks and programming languages. I'd like think I pick up new things quickly because I have a base understanding of common concepts between all of them and pick everything else up as I go. I think that's about it for me, hope you think the things on this page are interesting!
